Tomato and Basil Bruschetta


Jump to recipe


Fresh and delicious Tomato and Basil Bruschetta that is super flavorful, so easy to make and such a huge crowdpleaser when entertaining! Bruschetta is a classic Italian appetizer that is easy to make at home. Toasted bread is topped with a mixture of juicy tomatoes, garlic, Parmesan cheese and fresh basil. This top-rated bruschetta recipe proves that summertime gives you perfectly ripe and juicy tomatoes, one of the best ways to enjoy them is also one of the easiest.




Prep time: 5 minutes Cook time: 10 minutes Serving: 8 servings

INGREDIENTS
For the bruschetta:
  • 2 cups cherry, or grape tomatoes, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1/3 cup basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
For the baguette slices:
  • 1 large whole wheat baguette, sliced into 1/2-inch slices
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing
  • 1 clove garlic, sliced in half

INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. To make the bruschetta, slice the tomatoes and place in a bowl and mix in the chopped garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, basil, salt/pepper and set aside to let marinate.
  3. Slice the baguette into about 16 slices (or however many it yields) and lightly brush them with olive oil. Place each slice oiled-side down on a sheet pan and toast in the oven for 8 to 10 minutes, until lightly toasted.
  4. Once the bread is lightly toasted, rub garlic on each slice and spoon on the tomato mixture. Drizzle with the balsamic glaze, if desired, and enjoy!
